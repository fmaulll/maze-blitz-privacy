Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 30, 2025

TL;DR: Maze Blitz uses AdMob for ads (which collects device data), Firebase for analytics, and Google Play Games Services for cloud saves and leaderboards. We don't store your personal info. Game progress is saved locally or to your Google account if logged in.

1. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Data

  • Advertising Data: AdMob (Google) may collect:
    • Advertising ID (for personalized ads)
    • Device type/model
    • Device identifiers (e.g. Android Advertising ID)
    • IP address (general location)
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics (Google) may collect:
    • App usage information (e.g. session length, screens viewed)
    • Device and platform data (e.g. Android version, country)
    • Events like level completions, ads watched.
  • Game Progress: Saved locally on your device (not shared with us).

What We Don't Collect

  • Your name, email, or contact info
  • Precise GPS location
  • Payment information (no in-app purchases)

2. How We Use Data

  • Ads: Display relevant ads via AdMob
  • Analytics: Understand how players use the game to improve gameplay and performance (via Firebase)
  • Game Functionality: Save your progress locally

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: For advertisements
  • Google Firebase Analytics: For anonymous usage tracking and performance insights
  • Google Play Games Services: For sign-in, achievements, leaderboards, and cloud saves

These services may use collected data to:

  • Serve personalized ads (AdMob)
  • Understand app usage and engagement (Firebase)
  • Improve app stability and performance (if Crashlytics is used)

Learn more:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Data Collection
  • Firebase Privacy and Security
  • Play Games Services Privacy

We do not sell your personal data. Data collected is used only for the app's functionality, analytics, and ads, as outlined above.

4. User Controls

You can:

  • Reset Advertising ID in Android settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads via Google Ad Settings
  • Delete local game data by uninstalling the app

5. Children's Privacy

Maze Blitz complies with COPPA:

  • No personal data knowingly collected from children under 13
  • Ads shown are filtered for child-friendly content via AdMob

6. Data Deletion Request

If you'd like to request deletion of your game data (e.g. cloud save, leaderboard info), please email us at dazesh1treal@gmail.com. We’ll forward the request to the relevant third-party services (e.g. Google Play Games Services) if needed.

Changes will be posted here. Continued use means acceptance.
